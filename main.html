Welcome to the Kegonsa Seed Fund

Kegonsa Seed Fund I, LP is Wisconsin's premier seed venture capital fund. The Fund is managed by Kegonsa Capital Partners, LLC , a Wisconsin-based venture capital management firm. The primary Fund objective is investor IRR determined by GAAP and benchmarked to Cambridge and Associates venture capital index see Cambridge and Associates venture capital index. On January 15, 2010, the Kegonsa Seed Fund completed its investment period; at this time, the Fund is no longer making investments in new portfolio companies.
