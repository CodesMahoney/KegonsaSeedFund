Investment Strategy

The Money for Minnows investment strategy promotes new company creation by investing capital across diverse industries and technologies with a focus on the seed venture capital investment segment. The Money for Money investment criteria includes having the expected investor return being from company acquisition versus return of capital from initial public offerings in less than five years with the paid in capital of five to eight million dollars.
